About this page

(and my problematic hobby of making playlists)

Making weirdly specific spotify playlists based on random feelings is my (not so sectret) obsession. Here I share some of my favorite albums with you, clustered not by genre, artist or a specific decade, but by my own ambigious sentiments of vibe curation.

Go to the homepage and click on an album image to get started.
On the bottom of each page you will find some of my playlists that inspired this project.
