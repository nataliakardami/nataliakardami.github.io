About this page

(and my problematic hobby of making playlists)

Creating Spotify playlists based on unique, sometimes quirky feelings is my beloved hobby. Here, I don't categorize music by genre, artist, or era, but by the emotional journey each collection evokes. This space is dedicated to sharing these carefully curated albums and playlists with you.

How to Navigate

Homepage: Start your journey by visiting the homepage and clicking on an album cover. Each click reveals a collection designed to take you on a specific emotional or experiential ride.

Playlists Inspiration: At the bottom of every page, you'll find the playlists that inspired the content you're viewing. Feel free to explore and immerse yourself.

Navigation Bar: Use the navigation bar at the top of the page to jump directly to different sections and discover more of what this site has to offer.

Reccomendation feature (beta) By clicking the button on top of each gallery, you can get a random song recommendation
